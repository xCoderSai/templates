Providing Technology Credits to Nonprofits Around the World
 

The Amazon Web Services (AWS) Nonprofit Credit Program provides $2,000 in promotional credit annually to nonprofit organizations worldwide. Whether working to improve website performance, enhancing data practices through managed databases and analytics, propelling research through machine learning, or upgrading remote monitoring with the Internet of Things (IoT), we’re here to help.

Qualified nonprofits of all shapes and sizes—no matter the mission area—are encouraged to leverage the credit program to offset expenses associated with implementing cloud-based technology solutions. The AWS Nonprofit Credit Program helps organizations meet mission goals without upfront investment in physical infrastructure.
